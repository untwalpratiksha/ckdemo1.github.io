Automated Manufacturing

We have 3 state of the art manufacturing plant where we produce quality products with the help of modern technology and traditional Kolhapur regional recipe and ingredients.

Freezing Technology

Manufacturing facilities are equipped with blast freezing and rapid freezing technology this ensures color, texture flavor and nutrients are locked in to product. This state of art technology enable our customer to enjoy taste of freshly prepared product at outlet.

Refrigerated Transport

We own fleet of refrigerated transport vehicles to make sure efficient delivery of products without losing its natural qualities and freshness.

Laboratory

Our in house food laboratory dedicated to providing the best food analysis at the highest level of efficiency. At the forefront of analysis we bring expertise to the table for making all microbiological, chemical and sensor taste by using advanced technology in the industry.

Contamination detection

This system is installed at automated production line, to check any contamination of food products such as stone, metal, glass and high density plastic and rubber etc. This system detects contamination that cannot be detected by cameras, naked eye or metal detectors.
